Introduction: The Future of Engagement

EngageSense is more than a system—it’s a central Black Box for engagement data, blending inputs from sensors, wearables, and more. Building on the success of DJ Sense, EngageSense will serve as the core hub for engagement metrics across industries like education, sports, and events.

Our Vision

We envision creating a physigital processor, combining real-time physical and digital inputs into a universal Cost Per Engagement (CPE) metric that redefines how organizations measure interaction value.

Metrics & Use Cases: Measuring What Matters
